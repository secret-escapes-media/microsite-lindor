The Swiss Chocolatier

For almost 150 years, Swiss chocolatiers have been trying to uncover the secret family recipe that Rodolphe Lindt perfected all those years ago. From sourcing the finest quality cocoa beans, to pioneering the conching process that makes Lindt chocolate so irresistibly smooth and silky, Lindt chocolates are defined by the passion for innovation that the Lindt Master Chocolatiers embody. Lindt has become synonymous with famous Swiss chocolate, carved from Alpine heights and a penchant to create only the highest quality chocolate for you to enjoy.
