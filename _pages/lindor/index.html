Treat yourself to LINDOR bliss

Nothing speaks the language of indulgence better than a LINDOR chocolate truffle. Perfectly round, the Lindt milk chocolate gives way to an irresistibly smooth melting filling. Share this moment with someone, or keep it to yourself – each will guarantee a moment of bliss.
The Lindt Master Chocolatier

Since 1845 the Lindt Master Chocolatiers have been creating the ultimate chocolate delights. From sourcing the finest quality cocoa beans, to pioneering the conching process that makes Lindt chocolate so irresistibly smooth and silky, Lindt chocolates are defined by the passion for innovation that the Lindt Master Chocolatiers embody. Over 60 years ago the Lindt Master Chocolatier and his colleagues were busy creating delectable chocolate delights, and had no idea that they were about to come across something truly unique and as precious as the LINDOR chocolate truffle. The smooth melting centre has delivered moments of bliss ever since.
