The Lindt Master Chocolatier

Driven by their passion, dedication and innovative spirit since 1845, the Lindt Master Chocolatiers create the ultimate chocolate delights. From sourcing the finest quality cocoa beans, to pioneering the conching process that makes Lindt chocolate so irresistibly smooth and silky, Lindt chocolates are defined by the passion for innovation that the Lindt Master Chocolatiers embody. Over 60 years ago the Lindt Master Chocolatier and his colleagues were busy creating delectable chocolate delights, and had no idea that they were about to come across something truly unique and as precious as the LINDOR chocolate truffle.
