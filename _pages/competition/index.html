Win a romantic break in the home of Lindor

Secret Escapes and Lindor are offering you the chance to win an opulent stay in the home of the master chocolatiers.

Zürich is an enigmatic city where picturesque and effortlessly romantic medieval streets seamlessly wind into a startlingly modern European space. Its geography catalyses its stunning panorama, where its central river blends into a crystal lake shadowed by snowcapped peaks in the distance.

Kameha Grand

You’ll be staying at the Kameha Grand, a luxury stay perfect for a romantic getaway. The Deluxe room showcases elegant Swiss design, where you’ll be treated to breakfast each morning and a four-course ‘surprise menu’ one evening at the hotel’s michelin-starred YOU restaurant.

Zürich

Head into the centre of the city, a short 15 minute drive, and experience a masterclass in producing the most delicious chocolate in the world. You’ll enjoy a class with a Lindt master chocolatier, to set the tone of your opulent stay in Switzerland. At the Lindt Chocolateria in Kilchberg, you’ll learn about the history of chocolate, the Lindor difference and create your very own own pralines with your loved one.

Three nights of pure indulgence will be framed by a pair of business class tickets with the renowned SWISS, to jet you in comfort to and from your destination.
