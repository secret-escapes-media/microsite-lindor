The city

Paris is the most romantic city, without doubt (and I’m not just saying that because I’m French!) My husband and I loved the Passage des Panoramas, a covered walkway with cute restaurants and shops (close to the Hoxton Hotel), irresistibly charming and perfect for coffee and cake! The view from the top of Galeries Lafayettes is a beautiful spot to steal a kiss, followed by a walk along the Seine and the Canal St Martin hand-in-hand. Classics like lunch in a Parisian brasserie and coffee on the terrace of the Rue Montorgueil are firm favourites, as everything is just so picturesque! Paris is just irresistibly romantic.

The stay

Our stay complemented the elegant city perfectly. The Hoxton Hotel, newly opened, seems the place to be in Paris! Only a 15 minute walk from Gare du Nord and a 10 minute walk from Galeries Lafayette, it’s easy to get to the idyllic spots that make Paris such a great destination. The service was incredible – we were warmly welcomed and the staff catered to all our needs. I loved the designer interiors, the bar which had a cool vibe, their irresistible cocktails, and their massive Sunday brunch!

The chocolate

We were also lucky enough to be invited to Lindt Opera, Lindt’s flagship store in Paris. Whilst there, the Maitre Chocolatier let us carefully select our own ingredients to create a personalised chocolate bar just for us! We also chose from the delicious selection of LINDOR truffles and walked away with our own carefully selected pick & mix box. It was the perfect treat to sweeten our romantic getaway.
